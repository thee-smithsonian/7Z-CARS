About Us

7Z Cars was established in 2018 by our founder Mr. Larry James. He established this dealership due to his love for exotic cars and he wanted everyone to be able to drive the car of their dreams. He wanted to make dreams come true.

We import the most luxurious cars that money can buy because life is too short not to be driving your dream car.

7Z Cars sells the best of the best. Our cars are shipped directly from the factory in order to give you first hand experience of driving a brand new car. We deal directly with the different car manufaturers and this ensures that our customers can get the car of their dreams in their preferred specifications.

Our customers can order their cars with us and choose their preferred car specifications which we send to the desired car manufacturer.

You can also visit us at our showroom in Naguru and have a look at the different cars we have in stock. You can also visit us for consultation on which car brand and model would be best suited to you
